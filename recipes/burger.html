Burger Recipe

Description:

This burger is made with freshly grilled patties, melted cheese, crisp lettuce, and juicy tomatoes, all stacked in a soft bun. It's a perfect blend of flavors that will satisfy your taste buds!


Ingredients
  • Ground beef
  • Bun
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Condiments(ketchup,mustard,mayonaise)
  • salt and pepper

Steps
  1. Prepare Ingredients: Gather ground beef, buns, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape Patty: Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then form it into a round patty.
  3. Cook Patty: Grill or pan-fry the patty until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  4. Toast Buns: Lightly toast the buns on the grill or in a toaster for added flavor and texture.
  5. Add Cheese: Place a slice of cheese on top of the cooked patty to let it melt.
  6. Assemble Burger: On the bottom half of the bun, layer lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and any other desired toppings.
  7. Add Patty: Place the cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the toppings.
  8. Apply Condiments: Add ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise or any other desired sauces on top of the patty.
  9. Top with Bun: Close the burger with the top half of the bun.
  10. Serve: Your delicious burger is ready to be served and enjoyed! Optionally, serve with fries or a side of your choice.
Enjoy your homemade burger!
